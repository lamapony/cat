Post-launch playbook for Days 1-30 after ProductHunt. Goal: convert PH traffic into waitlist signups, pilot applications, and investor interest.

## Risk Mitigation

### If PH ranking drops below top 10

1. Activate all personal networks immediately (Dima, team, advisors)
2. Post in every relevant Slack/Discord community
3. Email entire contact list with personal ask
4. Ask advisors to share on their social accounts
5. Consider $50-100 paid Twitter/LinkedIn promotion

### If technical issues (site down, links broken)

1. Developer on standby — fix within 5 minutes
2. Post update on PH: "We're fixing [ISSUE], back in 5 min"
3. Redirect traffic to backup landing page if needed

### If negative comments

1. Reply within 10 minutes, always professionally
2. Acknowledge the concern specifically
3. Offer direct call or email: dmitrii@officecat.io
4. Never argue publicly
5. Log criticism as product improvement notes
