## Screenshot 1: Hero — "Meet Office Cat"

**Purpose:** First impression. Shows the product + emotional hook.

**Layout:**
- Background: Dark gradient (#0a0a0f to #12121a)
- Center: Robot cat device (3D render or high-quality photo)
  - LED face showing "happy" state (gentle pink pulse)
  - Small form factor, desk-friendly
- Top-left: Logo "Office Cat" + tagline "Social Catalyst for Teams"
- Bottom: Health bar (cyan #22d3ee, 100% full)
- Subtle glow effect around the cat (accent orange #ff6b35)

**Text overlay:**
- Headline: "A robot cat that fixes lonely offices"
- Subhead: "Not a productivity tool. A reason to talk to each other."

**Style:** Clean, modern, emotional. Like a premium hardware product page.

## Screenshot 2: The Problem — "Silent Offices"

**Purpose:** Establish the pain point. Relatable for office workers.

**Layout:**
- Split screen: Before / After
- **Left (Before):** Grey, desaturated office photo. People at desks, headphones on, no eye contact. Overlay: "40 people. Dead silence."
- **Right (After):** Warm, vibrant office. Two people walking, cat in hand, smiling. Overlay: "One cat. Real connection."
- Center divider: Office Cat device bridging the two sides
- Bottom stat bar: 3 metrics
  - "68% want better social tools at work"
  - "47% feel lonely in the office"
  - "$12B market, zero focused players"

**Style:** Editorial, documentary feel. Human and authentic.

---

## Screenshot 3: How It Works — "Walk to Heal"

**Purpose:** Show the core mechanic simply.

**Layout:**
- 3-step horizontal flow
- **Step 1 — Sense:** Cat on desk, LED face showing "stressed" (rapid red flash). Noise meter visible. Text: "The cat feels the office mood."
- **Step 2 — Fade:** Cat LED face dimming to grey, ears drooping. Health bar at 30%. Text: "When the team is stressed, the cat fades."
- **Step 3 — Heal:** Two colleagues walking down hallway, cat in one person's hand. LED face glowing cyan "healing" wave. Health bar climbing. Text: "A 10-minute walk heals the cat — and the team."
- Arrow connectors between steps
- Bottom: "No forced fun. Just natural moments."

**Style:** Instructional but warm. Like Apple product photography.

## Screenshot 4: Team Dashboard — "See Your Team Pulse"

**Purpose:** Show the SaaS value. Data-driven buyers (HR, managers) need this.

**Layout:**
- Dark UI dashboard mockup
- **Top:** Live cat status card — health 87%, mood "Happy", last walk 12 min ago
- **Left panel:** Activity feed
  - "Anna + Marcus took a walk — Cat healed +15%"
  - "Team quiet time: 2.3h today"
  - "Weekly walks: 12 (up 40%)"
- **Right panel:** Weekly report preview
  - Bar chart: Walks per day (Mon-Sun)
  - Mood trend line: Upward over 4 weeks
  - Quiet time insights: "Peak focus: 10am-12pm"
- **Bottom banner:** "Privacy-first. No individual tracking. Aggregate team data only."

**Style:** Clean dashboard UI. Similar to Notion/Linear aesthetic. Dark mode.
