# First 10 Commenters — ProductHunt Launch

## Strategy

ProductHunt's algorithm heavily weights early engagement. The first 10-20 comments create social proof and signal quality to the algorithm. We need a mix of:

1. **Founder network** — people who know us, will write genuine comments
2. **Target users** — office managers, HR people, startup founders
3. **Tech community** — builders who appreciate the hardware hack
4. **Investors** — seed-stage VCs who can add credibility

---

## Comment Templates (to send in advance)

```

what I'd build for v0.1 — enough to create emotional response without over-engineering.

The "fade" mechanic is clever. Turning social isolation into a *visual* problem 
(the cat looks sad) triggers action better than any dashboard metric.

Question: what's the battery life? And have you tested the walk-detection 
(motion sensor) accuracy in real offices?
```

```
relationship. I'd sign up for the pilot today.

One request: please make the cat face customizable. Our team would lose it 
over a seasonal Halloween cat.
```
