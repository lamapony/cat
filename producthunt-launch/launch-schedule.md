## Emergency Protocols

### If ranking drops below top 10
1. Activate all personal networks immediately
2. Post in every relevant Slack/Discord community
3. Email entire contact list
4. Ask advisors to share on their social
5. Consider small paid promotion ($50-100 on Twitter/LinkedIn)

### If technical issues (site down, links broken)
1. Fix immediately — have Developer on standby
2. Post update on ProductHunt: "We're fixing [ISSUE], back in 5 min"
3. Redirect traffic to backup landing page if needed

### If negative comments
1. Reply within 10 minutes, always professionally
2. Acknowledge the concern
3. Offer to jump on a call or email directly
4. Never argue publicly
5. Turn criticism into product improvement notes
