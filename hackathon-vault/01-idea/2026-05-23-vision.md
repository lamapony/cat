# Office Cat — Product Vision

## Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)

> "Office Cat is a shared digital pet for your team. It gets lonely when people don't talk, stressed when the office is too loud, and heals when colleagues take it for a walk together. It's not a toy — it's a social catalyst that creates natural excuses for people to interact, without forced team-building."

## Core Problem

**People don't socialize at work anymore.**

- Remote work killed spontaneous interaction
- Even in offices, people sit with headphones, eat alone, avoid eye contact
- Team-building feels forced and awkward
- Result: silos, burnout, quiet quitting

**Data:**
- 68% of employees want better social tools at work (Gallup)
- 47% feel isolated in open-plan offices (Harvard Business Review)
- Teams with strong social bonds are 50% more productive (MIT)

## Core Solution

**Office Cat creates natural excuses for people to interact.**

It's a shared digital pet that needs the ***team*** to survive:
- Gets lonely → someone notices → "Hey, the cat looks sad, let's walk it"
- Stressed by noise → team quiets down to help the pet
- Happy when team bonds → reflects collective emotional state

**Not:**
- ❌ A productivity tool
- ❌ A surveillance device
- ❌ A toy for entertainment

**Yes:**
- ✅ A social catalyst
- ✅ A shared responsibility
- ✅ A mood mirror for the team

## Emotional Loop

```
Team ignores each other
    ↓
Cat gets lonely (grey screen, droopy ears)
    ↓
Someone notices
    ↓
"Hey, the cat looks sad, want to take it for a walk?"
    ↓
Two colleagues walk and talk (10 minutes)
    ↓
Cat heals (glows, purrs)
    ↓
Team bonds
    ↓
Cat happy → Repeat
```

## Key Mechanics

| Mechanic | Trigger | Team Action | Outcome |
|----------|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Social Needs** | Low interaction | Walk the cat | Bonding |
| **Noise Sensing** | Loud environment | Quiet down | Calmer office |
| **Walk to Heal** | Cat is lonely | 10-min walk | Health + connection |
| **Mood Mirror** | Collective stress | Check in | Emotional awareness |
| **Shared Rituals** | Daily/weekly | Feed, clean | Recurring social moments |
| **Quiet Time** | After hours | Review report | Insights, no surveillance |

## Target User

**Primary:** Office managers, team leads, HR directors
**Secondary:** Employees who want more social connection
**Company size:** 10-100 employees
**Office type:** Open plan, hybrid, creative spaces
