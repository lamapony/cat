# Office Cat — Demo Script

## Setup (2 minutes before demo)
1. Charge Office Cat fully
2. Place on a desk in the office area
3. Ensure microphone is unobstructed
4. Test the health bar display
5. Have a colleague ready for the "walk" segment

---

## Demo Flow (5 minutes)

### 1. Introduction (30 sec)
**You say:** "Meet Office Cat — our team's digital pet. It's a Tamagotchi for the workplace. It feels noise, gets stressed, and needs us to take care of it. Built on a $99 Atech.dev kit."

**Action:** Office Cat shows happy face, health bar at 80%.

---

### 2. Loneliness (1 min)
**You say:** "Watch what happens when no one interacts with the cat."

**Action:** Leave Office Cat untouched for 30 seconds.

**Office Cat reacts:** Health bar ticks down from 80% to 60%. Screen tint shifts from warm to slightly grey. Ears droop slightly.

**You say:** "The cat is getting lonely. It's been sitting here, and no one has talked to it — or to each other. The cat reflects what the team feels."

**Technical:** Demonstrates inactivity detection, loneliness algorithm, health degradation.

---

### 3. The Fade (1 min)
**You say:** "If no one interacts, the cat starts to fade."

**Action:** Continue leaving Office Cat untouched for 30 seconds.

**Office Cat reacts:** Health drops below 30%. Screen goes grey. Movements slow. Ears fully drooped. Looks genuinely sad.

**You say:** "This is the 'lonely' state. The cat is fading because no one is talking to each other. It's a mirror — the team is isolated, and the cat shows it."

**Technical:** Demonstrates progressive health degradation, visual state transitions, emotional UI design.

---

### 4. Healing Walk (1.5 min)
**You say:** "The cat needs someone. And more importantly — the team needs each other. Who's up for a walk?"

**Colleague picks up Office Cat, turns to another person: "Want to come?"**

**Action:** Two people walk around the room for 30-60 seconds.

**Office Cat reacts:** Motion sensors detect movement. Proximity sensor detects two human signatures. Health bar climbs faster (2x speed for 2 people). Screen regains color. Ears perk up.

**Returns to desk:** Health at 90%. Happy purring animation. Screen glows warm orange.

**You say:** "Two colleagues just bonded. Not because of a forced activity — because a pet needed them. The cat didn't replace human interaction. It created a reason for it."

**Technical:** Demonstrates multi-person proximity detection, accelerated recovery for social interaction, social catalyst effect.

---

### 5. Team Mood Mirror (1 min)
**You say:** "Office Cat also reflects the collective mood."

**Action:** Show the weekly report on screen or phone app.

**Office Cat shows:** "This week: 4 healing walks. 6 unique interactions. Team social score: 8/10."

**You say:** "Not surveillance — just signals. HR sees 'team hasn't walked the cat in 3 days' and knows people aren't connecting. Not a productivity metric. A social pulse."

**Technical:** Demonstrates data aggregation, weekly reporting, privacy-first social analytics.

---

### 6. Wrap-up (30 sec)
**You say:** "Office Cat doesn't optimize your sprint velocity. It gives your team a reason to look up from their screens and talk to each other. $149. Shipping June 2026."

**Office Cat:** Happy face, full health bar, gentle purring animation.

---

## Backup Plans

### If noise demo fails:
- Use pre-recorded loud audio from phone
- Show "simulated noise" mode in settings

### If walk demo fails:
- Shake the cat gently to simulate motion
- Show "healing mode" button for demo purposes

### If screen/visuals fail:
- Use phone app as secondary display
- Describe what would happen while showing app
