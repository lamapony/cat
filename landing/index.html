Features

Everything your team needs to bond

Office Cat turns everyday office life into shared rituals that build genuine connections.

Walk to Heal

Stress & Recovery

Team Mood Mirror

How it works

From unboxing to your first healing walk

Ten minutes to set up. A lifetime of natural team connections.

Unbox & Place

Charge Office Cat, set it on a desk. It boots up, shows a happy face, and starts listening to the room.
