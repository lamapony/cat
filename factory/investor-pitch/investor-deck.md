### 3. The Solution — Office Cat

A robot cat that lives in the office and gets lonely when ignored.

**Core mechanic:**
1. Cat's health drops when left alone
2. Someone notices, invites a colleague for a walk
3. Motion + proximity sensors restore health
4. Two people talk. Bond. Return as a team.

**Why it works:**
- No apps to download
- No meetings to schedule
- No manager enforcement
- Emotional trigger (suffering pet) > rational trigger (KPI dashboard)

---

### 4. How It Works (3-Step Flow)

```
CONNECT → FADE → HEAL
   ↓        ↓       ↓
Cat needs   Health   Two people walk
human       drops    the cat for 10 min
interaction below 30%  → health restores
                        → conversation happens
```

**Hardware:** Atech.dev base + custom firmware + motion/proximity sensors
**Software:** Real-time health sync, team analytics dashboard, multi-cat fleet management
