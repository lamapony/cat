# Office Cat — 50-Office Pilot Program

## Program Overview

**Goal:** Recruit 50 pilot offices by June 15, 2026. Target 80% retention.

**Incentive:** Free hardware ($149 value) + 6 months SaaS ($29/mo = $174 value). Total per pilot: $323 value at zero cost.

**Selection Criteria:**
- 10–100 employees
- Open-plan or hybrid layout
- Willing to provide feedback (weekly check-ins, monthly survey)
- Decision-maker reachable (office manager, HR lead, founder)

---

## Target Segments (Priority Order)

### 1. Tech Startups (Priority: HIGH)
- **Why:** Early adopters, open-plan culture, founder-accessible, rapid feedback cycles
- **Where:** YC network (W25, S25 batches), Product Hunt community, Indie Hackers, local startup hubs (Copenhagen, Berlin, Amsterdam)
- **Hook:** "Your team is building the future — give them a companion that makes the office feel alive."
- **Count target:** 20 pilots

### 2. Creative Agencies (Priority: HIGH)
- **Why:** Culture-sensitive, design-aware, word-of-mouth multipliers
- **Where:** Dribbble/Behance networks, agency directories (Awwwards, Clutch), LinkedIn creative directors
- **Hook:** "The only office pet that matches your aesthetic and doesn't shed on the mood boards."
- **Count target:** 15 pilots

### 3. Enterprise HR / Workplace Teams (Priority: MEDIUM)
- **Why:** Budget holders, scalability proof point, case study goldmine
- **Where:** LinkedIn HR leaders, workplace experience conferences (CoreNet, GWA), Slack communities (PeopleGeeks, Resources for Humans)
- **Hook:** "Pilot with 1 team. Scale to 50 floors. Data-backed wellbeing ROI in 30 days."
- **Count target:** 10 pilots

### 4. Coworking Spaces (Priority: MEDIUM)
- **Why:** High foot traffic, visible product placement, multi-tenant feedback
- **Where:** WeWork community managers, local coworking associations, FlexOffice network
- **Hook:** "The amenity that makes members stay longer and talk about you."
- **Count target:** 5 pilots

## Application Form (Google Typeform)

Fields:
1. Company name & website
2. Team size (open-plan count)
3. Industry (startup/agency/enterprise/coworking)
4. Office location (city/country)
5. Current office culture initiatives (none/some/many)
6. Why Office Cat? (open text — quality signal)
7. Commitment to feedback (yes/no)
8. Decision-maker name & role
9. Contact email
10. Preferred start date

**Scoring:**
- Team size 10–100: +3 pts
- Open-plan confirmed: +2 pts
- Willing to feedback: +2 pts
- Creative/tech industry: +2 pts
- Detailed "why" answer: +1 pt
- **Threshold:** 7+ pts = auto-accept, 5–6 pts = review, <5 pts = waitlist

## Pilot Agreement (1-Page)

**We provide:**
- 1× Office Cat hardware unit (free, $149 value)
- 6 months SaaS access (free, $174 value)
- Onboarding call (30 min)
- Weekly check-in (Slack/email, 5 min)
- Monthly survey link (Typeform, 3 min)

**You provide:**
- Place unit in common area (not closet)
- Share feedback weekly for 6 weeks, then monthly
- Allow case study mention (anonymized or named — your choice)
- Option to purchase at $149/unit + $29/mo after 6 months

**Exit:** Return hardware anytime. No cost. No lock-in.
